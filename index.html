    The Art Agora - Virtual Gallery & Apparel Shop

    view website

    Description: Creator of a virtual gallery that showcases artwork from underrepresented artists around the globe. The platform also includes a shop where these artworks are transformed into fine print designs available on a variety of products, with a percentage of each sale going to charity.

    • Virtual Art Gallery: Developed an immersive online gallery experience where visitors can explore and engage with artwork in a digital space, supporting emerging and underrepresented artists.
    • Apparel & Product Design: Curated artwork and collaborated with artists to create print designs for t-shirts, hoodies, tote bags, mugs, picture frames, and sweatshirts.
    • E-commerce Integration: Implemented a shopping section where users can purchase artwork in fine print form, creating a bridge between art curation and fashion.
    • Charity Support: A percentage of every sale is donated to charitable organizations, focusing on causes like art education, environmental protection and supporting marginalized communities.
    • Custom Design Tools: Integrated user-friendly customization options, allowing customers to choose specific artwork for their preferred product type (e.g., custom tote bag or mug with a chosen art print).
    • Artist Promotion & Empowerment: The platform not only sells apparel but also promotes artists by linking each design to the original creator's profile, raising awareness of their work.
